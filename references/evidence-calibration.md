# Evidence Calibration

## Goal

Match claim strength to the exact control, artifact, and robustness scope actually evaluated.

## Rewrite rule

When a sentence sounds like it has eliminated an explanation, ask:

1. What was the exact control?
2. What alternative explanation was held fixed?
3. Is the conclusion universal, or only inside that control?

If the support is local, make the sentence local.

## Preferred patterns

- "Within this binary-IID control, ..."
- "Across the evaluated mechanism-family holdouts, ..."
- "In the observed sample, ..."
- "Under the correlated-observation stress test, ..."
- "For the declared partition family, ..."

## Overclaim patterns to soften

- "rules out" when the evidence comes from one control or one modeling family;
- "proves" or "establishes" when the result is empirical rather than formal;
- "generalizes" when transfer only failed or succeeded on a narrow holdout family;
- "shows that X is the explanation" when the evidence only localizes the dominant loss or compatible mechanism.

## Safer substitutes

- "localizes the dominant loss to ..."
- "is consistent with ..."
- "supports the interpretation that ..."
- "within this control, the reduction points to ..."
- "the evaluated holdouts do not support transfer beyond ..."
